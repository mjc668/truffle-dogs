## Why Truffle Dogs?

Dogs have an incredible sense of smell - up to 100,000 times more sensitive than humans. This makes them the perfect tool for locating truffles buried underground.

Our approach focuses on:

- **Welfare** - Positive, reward-based methods only
- **Trust** - Building strong bonds between dog and handler  
- **Teamwork** - Creating reliable partnerships that last
