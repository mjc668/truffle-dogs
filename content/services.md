## Training Services

### Truffle Dog Foundation Training

Start your dog's search journey! Build confidence, curiosity, and independent searching using food or toy rewards in a calm, supportive environment.

**What's included:**

- Initial assessment of your dog's suitability
- Foundation scent work skills
- Introduction to truffle odor
- Progress tracking and support

### Truffle Dog Continuing Training

Ongoing learning at your dog's pace. Flexible training sessions that adapt to your schedule and your dog's development.

**What's included:**

- Advanced scent detection exercises
- Real-world orchard training
- Problem-solving and troubleshooting
- Seasonal readiness preparation

### Custom Training Programs

Tailored training programs designed specifically for your needs, whether you're a hobby farmer or running a commercial truffle operation.
